Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Habibi Hostel and Restaurant in Unawatuna, Sri Lanka collects, uses, and protects your personal data.

Information We Collect

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Country and address
  • Passport details (upon check-in only)

Use of Information

Your personal data is used for booking management and to comply with Sri Lankan government requirements. We store this data securely in our PMS and a government-required guest registration document.

Security

We take data protection seriously and ensure your data is stored securely and only accessible by authorized staff.
